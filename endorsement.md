# endorsement

Endorsement names who stands behind a statement.

An endorsed statement is operator-owned.

An unendorsed statement is machine-owned until an operator takes it on.

The machine never endorses for the operator.

Ownership and truth are separate. Ownership says who may responsibly change a statement; evidence says whether it still holds.

An intent is operator-owned or machine-owned. The material that corresponds to it answers to whichever stands behind it.
