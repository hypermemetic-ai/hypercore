# role-assembly — two maximally-skilled roles, one source, derived channels

Item 2 (ADR 0009, ratified). Assemble the architect (Opus 4.8 via `claude -p`) and the worker
(GPT-5.5 via pi/OMP), each maximally specialized, from the repo documents (the single source)
feeding three derived channels: a **minimal shared `AGENTS.md`** (non-inferable operational lines
only, reached by a derived `CLAUDE.md` bridge), on-demand **skills** (the routing), and the per-episode
**prompt**. The worker keeps the whole spec preloaded by construction (the slice-4 keystone);
just-in-time is reserved for the ADR/reference tail. Skills + the agents file are derived renders
regenerated by the fold — no hand-copy (this is what retired the `worker.DEPTH` smell).

**All steps are folded.** The pre-seam channels (1–4b) and the worker-fenced OMP seam (5–6 —
the worker runs at its fence, pulls the ADR/reference tail just-in-time, and is summoned as GPT via
`omp`) are built; the live loading is the watched experiment, never faked into the harness.

## folding condition

The channels are assembled and single-sourced, regenerated by the fold; the worker carries the
whole spec preloaded; the worker-fenced side is **built** — its model transport runs at the fence
(cwd = its worktree), the ADR/reference tail is pulled just-in-time from the checkout, and the
worker is flipped to GPT via `omp` (a different model from the architect, ADR 0009). The live
loading — `omp` auto-loading the fence's anchor and discovering its skills — is watched evidence the
first autonomous run verifies, never faked into the harness (the honest limit, slice-7-F1).

  (`engine/depth.py`); the `depth` skill artifact materializes at `skills/depth/SKILL.md`; slice 10

- **step 4 — the architect's methodology skills.** `engine/methodology.py` renders the architect's
