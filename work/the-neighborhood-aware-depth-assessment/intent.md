The neighborhood-aware depth assessment — when the depth gate trips at a fold, the architect should present a reasoned depth/length assessment alongside the decision, read across the flagged file's neighborhood, so the operator settles well-informed instead of against a bare line-count.

Today the depth gate raises a length decision but the architect forwards the gate's mechanical template untouched: `communication.integrate` takes `conditions._depth_decision`'s string ("N lines, past the 400-line signal — re-cut / deepen / accept") and raises it verbatim, adding no reasoning of its own. So the operator settles against a line-count and three verbs, and the deliberation collapses to the myopic binary — shorten the addition, or accept the length. The length ratchet's purpose was the opposite: a length past the signal is meant to *invite architectural reflection* and surface opportunities for rearchitecture. A mechanical, file-local template cannot carry that, so the value is lost at the moment it should be delivered.

Build it so that when the depth gate trips, the architect runs a reasoned depth/length assessment of the flagged file *read in its neighborhood* — its callers, its siblings, the cross-module boundaries where a misplaced responsibility would show — and raises that assessment, with a lean, on the operator's queue in place of the bare template. The operator still settles (approve / cut / explain); the assessment informs the call, it does not make it, and it does not gate the fold (the depth condition already holds it). The neighborhood is seen by *consulting the standing architecture-review's whole-tree read* — the structural map and complexity debt it already computes — not by spinning up a second whole-tree scan, extending hypercore's existing "one criterion, two scopes" shape (the per-tree gate at the fold, the standing whole-tree scan) from the raw length number to the model-judged depth assessment. The assessment attaches only to the *escalating-guard* decision the operator settles (depth, and by the same logic the vocabulary guard), never to the flat, never-waveable refusals (the delta does not apply, the provenance no-trail), which must not be dressed as negotiable.

To surface in grilling: how this composes with the already-filed `model-driven-depth-scan` ask — distinct asks sharing one depth-assessment seam (one criterion judged in one place, triggered two ways: reactively at the fold, proactively over the standing tree) versus folding this into that build, with no second copy of the depth standard to drift. The concrete seam the architect is fed (the review's rendered map and debt, plus the flagged file's content and diff) and how the gate must *type* its outcomes so only the depth decision triggers the assessment: `conditions.unmet` today flattens every unmet condition to one opaque string, so the escalating decision and the flat refusal are indistinguishable to the integrate path. Whether the assessment is purely advisory prose (like the coherence say) or must leave a watched-evidence trace on the node — lean advisory, since it informs a settlement the operator records, and the accepted-length record already carries its own provenance trail. And that the assessment carries a lean and a flip — the architect's recommendation and the one thing that would change it — matching the grilling card and the rest of the architect's voice.
