## the surfacing bar gets written down
- ask: redrafted on your words (general, concrete, consistent under pressure). the meaning: you delegate execution, never stakes — the machine decides how, you decide anything that changes your world. the floor: five yes/no tests on the act, checked at one named moment — before the first write or command that creates the material. surface as a decision any act that (1) leaves the project folder — sends, publishes, installs, spends; (2) touches what the operator owns or uses — their words, their files, their environment; (3) binds future sessions — a default, a policy, a recurring job, a dependency; (4) takes more than one git command or one redo to take back; (5) or draws internal argument about whether any of these apply — doubt surfaces. judgment may surface more; the floor is what no model can miss, and every commit can be audited against it after the fact
- options: go — the floor lands as a [machine] amendment to rule 6 and the skill, through ratification like any statement; redirect — name what the floor still misses; cut — the bar stays judgment alone, and weaker models inherit the mush
