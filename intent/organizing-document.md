# organizing document

The methodology root keeps its intent in seven segments:

- **foundations**: why hypercore exists and the properties it protects.
- **structure**: nodes, material, segments, and parent-child reach.
- **statements**: what statements are and what makes them worth keeping.
- **collaboration**: the operator-machine relation at the level of roles and judgment.
- **endorsement**: who stands behind statements.
- **sweep**: semantic coherence across written claims.
- **decomposition**: splitting large meaning across real child nodes.

Each segment is written at `intent/<segment>.md`.

This document names the current division. It is not a taxonomy for every future node.
